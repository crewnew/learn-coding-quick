Why IT Recruitment?

More and more programmers and IT personnel is needed in our World as more and more things get automated and AI-driven. There's is already a huge demand for the top programmers and companies are finding it hard to fill the positions they need. Thus more and more IT recruiters are needed.

IT recruiter gets to learn the basics of modern technologies and will get to speak to many different IT professionals during the initial interviews. It is a great starting point to become a technical project manager for example in near future.
