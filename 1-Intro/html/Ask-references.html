Ask for a contact to get a reference

Even better if the reference comes with a brilliant piece of code.

Always be clear on exactly what you need. Like that you are avoiding the back-and-forth messaging and saving time. The quicker the hiring process the higher the chances that the candidate gives up and the customer gets upset. Ask for: Full name and job title of the reference. Necessary contact information for reference.

aText: .refcan (reference from the candidate)

Your code looked nice and we're happy to move forward with you. Thank you very much for your efforts so far! We’d like to move to the final stage now so could you provide please me with preferably 2-3 references of yours? It would be nice if you could send me the names, reporting relationships and contact details of your former managers/colleagues.

Ask the reference

aText: .refbos (reference from the previous boss)

Subject line: Could you provide a reference for [Candidate_name]?

Hello [Name],

My name is [your name] and I work for CrewNew Ltd. We’re in the process of hiring for a [job title] and one of your former colleagues, [Candidate_name], has successfully passed all our recruiting phases.

Before we make the final decision, I’d like to ask for some information to ensure we’re making the right choice. I’m reaching out to you because [Candidate_name] pointed to you as a potential reference. It’d be very useful if you could tell me a bit about your overall experience working with [Candidate_name] and whether you’d recommend the co-operation.

Could I call you for a brief discussion today or tomorrow? If you’d rather send me information via email, feel free to do so.

Of course, our communication is confidential.

Thank you for your help,

[Email signature]
